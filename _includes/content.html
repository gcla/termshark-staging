Features

  • Read pcap files or sniff live interfaces.
  • Use Wireshark's display filters.
  • Reassemble TCP and UDP streams.
  • View conversations by protocol.
  • Written in Go - for Linux, macOS, FreeBSD, Android (termux) and Windows.
